Current methods of finding a coffee shop with reliably good espresso drinks fall woefully short.

Large market review sites are flooded with subjective reviews that miss the target. Many times the reviewer isn't even commenting on the quality of the coffee.

Coffee industry websites and food and culture websites are a little better in terms of quality reviews; however, most are in depth reviews that highlight one or two coffee shops over the course of many months or even years.

Extracted is focused on providing users with up to date, highly verified information: what coffee shops deilver consistent high quality espresso drinks.
