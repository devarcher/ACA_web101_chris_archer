How this works

Welcome!

Our mission is to locate and share all of the best espresso in the United States.

The inital 500 invitations to join the app will be given through existing users to ensure that only seasoned baristas who know great espresso will be able to nominate and verify coffee shops.

Join your friends

Share your knowledge and benefit by gaining access to our growing list of great coffee shops around the country!

Share Your Favorite Shops

Once you've joined, nominate any of your favorite shops that aren't on the list. It's ok to be a tough critic. We only want the best.

Shop Verification

Once a shop has been nominated, it will go through a verfication and review process.

This is to ensure only shops that consisntently serve the best espress drinks make the cut. There are no ratings for shops. Either they are in or out.

Tell somone new

If you know baristas that know their business who live in other towns, let them know about the APP!

We want to populate as much of the US as we can to help as many people as we can to find great coffee!

Enjoy!

Any time you head to a new city, check out the app to find amazing coffee. No more sorting through yelp reviews!
